IT Security Suspension Policies

Suspending an account revokes the employee's login credentials immediately and marks their status as SUSPENDED. Active manager accounts cannot be suspended if there are employees reporting directly to them; reassign the reporting lines through HR before initiating suspension.

System Safeguard: Under no circumstances may an IT Support user suspend or disable an HR department account.
